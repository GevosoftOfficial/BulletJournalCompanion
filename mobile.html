FEATURES

The Bullet Journal Companion Is Full Of Features And Is Constantly Improved & Updated.

  • Simple Design

    Uses A Clean And Simple UI Without Any Clutter Or Unnecessary Potions, It Simply Just Works.

  • Sidebar

    A Sidebar That Is Accessible From Everywhere With A Simple Swipe. The Sidebar View's Your Log's And Has Quick Navigations Buttons To Help You Quickly Navigate The App.

  • Text, Sound & Drawing Notes

    You Can Store Text, Sound & Drawing Notes All In One Place.

  • Reminders

    You Can Set Reminders In The App To Ex. Remind You To Reflect.

  • Store Important Pages Digitally

    Store Your Most Important Pages In The App With A Button Click. You Will Never Have To Flip Through All Of Your Bujo's Indexes Just To Find One Page.

  • Free To Download & Use!

    To Download And Use The App Is Completely Free, But You Can Always Support The Development By Donating.
