Journal Log Companion

If you only need the log


ABOUT

Have a place to write down your logs when you don't have access to your journal.

- The Log Companion keeps track of your logs for you. - Uses common bullets/signifiers like Tasks, Notes, and Events + Migration (>) and scheduling (<) symbols. - Features a quick and user-friendly UI. - No clutter it simply just works.

The Log Companion comes from the developers behind the first Android released Journal Companion App and functions as a more basic alternative with just the log and nothing more. If you want more functionality download the free Journal Companion app by Gevosoft.
